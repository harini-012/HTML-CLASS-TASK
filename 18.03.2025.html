About emerging technology

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing multiple sectors in India, from healthcare and education to agriculture and governance. The Indian government has launched several initiatives, such as the IndiaAI Mission and the National AI Strategy by NITI Aayog, to promote AI research and development. AI-driven solutions are helping farmers predict crop yields, improving diagnostics in hospitals, and enhancing efficiency in public services through automation. Indian startups are also making significant contributions, developing AI-powered chatbots, financial tools, and personalized learning platforms.

Challenges

hallenges like data privacy, ethical concerns, and job displacement need to be addressed for sustainable AI adoption. As India moves towards becoming a global AI hub, the integration of responsible AI practices will be crucial in ensuring inclusive growth and innovation.
